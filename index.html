Nikola Tesla

1856 - 1943

"The present is theirs; the future, for which I really worked is mine."

Biography

Nikola Tesla was a Serbian-American inventor, electrical engineer, mechanical engineer and futurist best known for his contribution to the design of the modern alternating current(AC) electricity supply system.

achievements

  • Developed the alternating current(AC) electrical system.
  • Invented the Tesla coil.
  • Contributed to the developement of x-ray technology.
  • Innovations in wireless communication and energy transfer.
